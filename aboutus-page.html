OUR HISTORY:

Our brand was created by Theodor Tobler, a Swiss chocolatier. His father, Jean, opened a chocolate shop in Bern, Switzerland, in 1868. Then in 1900 Theodor took over the company. 8 years later in 1908, Theodor and his cousin Emil invented the signature Toblerone bar. For the name, they merged the surname Tobler with “torrone,” the Italian word for nougat.
